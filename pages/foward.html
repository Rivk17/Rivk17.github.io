Foward

Foward it`s an website builed with Bootstrap, done for a UX/UI class I had, meant to help people choose a carrier by providing a site where profesionals gather and answer questions with real knowledge, knowledge they have after years of work in their areas.

It goes after two kinds of people:
1)Those who still don`t know what carrier to pursue, and need help deciding.
2)Profesionals who look for a site in which to show their knowledge and generate connections in their filds of work.

  • Javascript
