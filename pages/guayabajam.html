Guayaba Jam

Guayaba Jam it`s a project in development, in which Carolina Loaiza, Tatiana Bonilla and myself are working on. I was the one who made the design and HTML/CSS of the gig. The basic idea is to provide a place where costarrican musicians and music-goers can gather around and buy, sell and share music, to increase the legal purchases of costarrican music.
